Automating in-class competitions

This attack/defend class is structured to be divided into 3 rotating teams: blue team, red team, and white team. White deploys, red attacks, and blue defends their respective virtual machines. There is a fourth role which is usually 1 person, which is the back-end/DevOps guy. I figure I'd try something new, so I signed up for the back-end role.

So my partner and I don't have to show up to class anymore invest more time in other projects! Do more with less, right?

I wrote about a dozen scripts for this class but I'll be discussing the one I'm proudest of - the script I wrote to automate a competition so my partner and I didn't have to repeat process we go through during a competition in class.
